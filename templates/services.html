AVAILABLE SERVICES




Your lashes, your routine, your peace 🤍


Luxurylash extensions in Nashville. Hybrid, Classic, and Volume sets by a licensed esthetician. Long-lasting retention, lash baths included for members. Book your glam at Faithfully Lashed Studio.


Classic Full Set

Enhance your natural beauty with our Classic Full Set. One lightweight lash extension is applied to each natural lash, creating a soft, natural, and polished look that’s perfect for everyday wear. Ideal for first-time lash clients or those who prefer a subtle enhancement.


Hybrid Full Set

Get the best of both worlds with our Hybrid Full Set — a beautiful blend of classic and volume techniques. Hybrid lashes add texture, fluff, and extra fullness while still looking soft and wispy. Perfect for clients who want more drama than classics but not as bold as full volume.


Volume Full Set

For the lash lovers who want bold, glamorous eyes ✨ Our Volume Full Set uses ultra-lightweight extensions in customized fans to create a fluffy, dense lash line. Perfect for clients who want maximum fullness while keeping a soft, airy finish.


Mega Volume Full Set

Turn up the intensity with our darkest, most dramatic style. Mega Volume lashes use ultra-fine extensions to create dense, bold fans for a striking, high-impact look. Perfect for special occasions or clients who love a show-stopping lash line every day.


Lash Lift & Tint

Say goodbye to curling wands and mascara. Our Lash Lift enhances your natural lashes by curling them upward, while a Tint darkens them for a bold, effortless look that lasts 6–8 weeks. A perfect low-maintenance option.


Brow Stain

Add depth and definition to your brows with our Brow Stain treatment. Longer-lasting than traditional tint, this service enhances your natural brow color and shape, giving you a bold, filled-in look that can last up to 2 weeks on skin and 6 weeks on hair.


Brow Lamination

Achieve sleek, sculpted, and fluffy brows that stay in place all day. Brow lamination straightens and sets your natural brows for a fuller, more defined, editorial finish that lasts up to 6 weeks.


Deluxe Brow Treatment

The ultimate brow transformation. Our Deluxe Brow Treatment combines lamination, stain, and brow wax for perfectly lifted, tinted, and sculpted brows. Ideal for clients who want a full, luxury brow makeover in one appointment.

Cancellation Policy❗

At Faithfully Lashed, I value both your time and mine. As a solo artist, every appointment is specially reserved just for you. To ensure fairness and respect for all clients, the following cancellation policy is in place:


📅 Cancellation Notice

  • I kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
    •
  • Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will result in loss of your $25 deposit.
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows may be charged up to 50% of the full service price.

💬 How to Cancel

  • Cancellations must be made via text or through the booking system.
  • If you’re feeling sick or experiencing an emergency, please communicate with me as soon as possible. I understand life happens, but communication is key.

🚫 No-Show Policy

  • No-call/no-shows will result in forfeiture of your deposit and may require full prepayment for future bookings or result in being declined future services.

*No guests or extra company during service

By booking your appointment, you agree to this cancellation policy. Thank you for respecting my time and supporting my small business 💕
